# Mandarin Pronunciation Learning Tool - User Guide

## üìñ Overview
This tool helps Mandarin language teachers create master pronunciation lessons and allows students to practice and receive AI-powered feedback on their pronunciation.

## üéØ Key Features

### For Teachers:
- **Create Master Lessons**: Record audio samples for sentences, words, or vowels
- **Organize Content**: Save and manage multiple lessons
- **Metadata Storage**: Each lesson includes title, type, text/pinyin, and audio

### For Students:
- **Practice Mode**: Select lessons and practice pronunciation
- **AI Analysis**: Get detailed feedback from Claude AI
- **Scoring System**: Receive scores out of 100 based on pronunciation quality
- **Progress Tracking**: View statistics and practice history
- **Instant Feedback**: Real-time analysis and improvement suggestions

## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Teacher Setup (Creating Lessons)

1. **Open the Tool**: Open `mandarin_pronunciation_tool.html` in your web browser
2. **Select Teacher Mode**: Click the "Teacher Mode" button
3. **Create a Lesson**:
   - Enter a lesson title (e.g., "Basic Greetings")
   - Select lesson type (Sentence, Word, or Vowel)
   - Enter the Mandarin text and pinyin
   - Click "Start Recording" and speak clearly
   - Click "Stop Recording" when done
   - Review your recording
   - Click "Save Lesson"

**Tips for Teachers:**
- Record in a quiet environment
- Speak clearly and at a natural pace
- Use proper tones and pronunciation
- Keep recordings concise (5-30 seconds)

### 2. Student Practice

1. **Select Student Mode**: Click the "Student Mode" button
2. **Choose a Lesson**: Click on any lesson from the list
3. **Listen to Master**: Play the master recording multiple times
4. **Record Your Attempt**:
   - Click "Start Recording"
   - Repeat what you heard
   - Click "Stop Recording"
5. **Get Analysis**: Click "Analyze My Pronunciation"
6. **Review Feedback**:
   - View your score (0-100)
   - Read AI-generated feedback
   - Check detailed metrics
   - Review improvement suggestions

## üìä Understanding the Scoring System

### Score Breakdown:
- **85-100**: Excellent - Near-native pronunciation
- **70-84**: Good - Clear communication with minor issues
- **55-69**: Fair - Needs improvement in specific areas
- **Below 55**: Needs Practice - Focus on fundamentals

### Metrics Analyzed:
1. **Tone Accuracy**: Correctness of the four Mandarin tones
2. **Clarity**: How clear and understandable the pronunciation is
3. **Rhythm & Pacing**: Natural flow and timing

## üíæ Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser:
- **Lessons**: Stored in browser localStorage
- **Practice History**: Tracked per device
- **Audio Files**: Converted to base64 and stored locally

**Note**: Data is device-specific and won't transfer between devices unless you export it.

## üîç Troubleshooting

### Microphone Not Working:
- Check browser permissions
- Ensure microphone is connected
- Try refreshing the page
- Check system sound settings

### Recording Not Saving:
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure sufficient storage space
- Try a different browser

### AI Analysis Not Working:
- Check internet connection
- Wait a moment and retry
- Review fallback feedback if AI unavailable

### Lessons Not Appearing:
- Ensure lessons were saved properly
- Check that you're in the correct mode
- Try refreshing the page
- Check browser localStorage
